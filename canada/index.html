Why Indian Students Choose Canada

Affordable tuition vs. USA/UK

Study at world-class institutions without breaking the bank. Canadian education offers exceptional value compared to other English-speaking countries.

Up to 3-year PGWP

Post-Graduation Work Permit allows you to gain valuable Canadian work experience and build your career after studies.

High visa success rate

Canada welcomes international students with streamlined visa processes and high approval rates for genuine applicants.

Easy PR opportunities

Multiple pathways to permanent residency through programs like CEC, PNP, and Express Entry system.

Part-time work & internships

Work up to 20 hours per week during studies and unlimited hours during breaks to support your finances and gain experience.
